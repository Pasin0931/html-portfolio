Fried egg

Ingtedients and tools

Ingtedients

  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Any topping that you prefer

Tools

  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Stove (Any kind)

Instruction

  1. Crack your prepared egg into any container.
  2. Add your prefered topping into the container.
  3. Stir it until your egg and topping have mixed together.
  4. Pour cooking oil into the pan.
  5. Heat up the pan, by adjusting the stove temperature.
  6. Pour your stired egg into the heated pan.
  7. Cook it until the texture looks golden and crusty.
  8. Flip it and do the same. (Causion : Don't over cook it !)
  9. Your fried egg is ready to eat.
